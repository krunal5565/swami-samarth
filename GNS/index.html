Who Are We

Goenchi Nari Shakti (GNS) is a Goan organization uniting Goans in the cause of advancing peace and well-being.

With a mission to provide help to needy, Goechi Nari Shakti Foundation was started in the year 2018 in Goa and is currently under the chairmanship of Shri. Nisha Vernekar. GNS has gone beyond in reaching out to those who need their support and help.

Unity

Works to empower individuals and families to lead happier with healthier lives, and to implement community development and unity.

Platform

A platform for selfless service for uplifting the helpless and needy and creating joyful life of all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build healthy individuals, families, societies, and nation.

Our Cause

Goechi Nari Shakti serve as a thriving model for human empowerment and community revitalization throughout the world.
